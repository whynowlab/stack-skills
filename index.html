// the problem

Your AI is
thinking wrong.

Not wrong answers. Wrong process. Out-of-the-box AI has hard-coded cognitive biases that no prompt can fix.

⚡
First-answer bias

Your AI picks the first plausible answer, not the best one. It optimizes for appearing helpful over being correct.

🪞
Sycophancy trap

It agrees when it should push back. Tell it your idea is great — it'll find reasons to confirm that, even if you're wrong.

🌫
Research theater

It researches by guessing, not verifying. Confident-sounding outputs with no source validation or tier grading.

// 7 meta-cognitive skills

The complete
stack.

Each skill is a cognitive module. Install one. Install all. Stack them together.

01 / 07
cross-verified-research

4-stage research pipeline with source tier grading (S/A/B/C). Cross-references claims across independent sources before committing to any output.

RESEARCH
02 / 07
creativity-sampler

5 probability-weighted options that fight typicality bias. Scores each option by novelty and feasibility before surfacing the unconventional winner.

DECISION
03 / 07
adversarial-review

3-vector Devil's Advocate attack on any decision or plan. Must find real problems — cannot return a clean pass without justification.

REVIEW
04 / 07
deep-dive-analyzer

Microscopic deconstruction of complex systems across 5 analytical lenses. Surface assumptions, hidden dependencies, and edge cases at depth.

ANALYSIS
05 / 07
skill-composer

Multi-skill pipeline orchestration. Chain and compose skills into sequential or parallel workflows for complex, multi-stage cognitive tasks.

META
06 / 07
persona-architect

Context-aware AI persona design framework. Builds structured personas with defined reasoning styles, constraints, and cognitive specializations.

META
07 / 07
tiered-test-generator

Knowledge verification at multiple difficulty tiers — L1 recall to L9 synthesis. Exposes gaps in understanding that simple Q&A cannot detect.
